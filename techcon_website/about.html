History

TECHCON began as a small academic-industry forum and has grown into the flagship conference of the Semiconductor Research Corporation. Over the years, it has evolved into a platform that connects
emerging researchers with top engineers and thought leaders in semiconductor innovation

Mission

TECHCON aims to drive progress in semiconductor technology through collaboration, innovation, and diversity. It brings together researchers, students,and industry leaders to exchange knowledge, inspire innovation,
and prepare the next generation of talent
