TECHCON 2024 took place from September 8–10, 2024, at the Renaissance Hotel in Austin, Texas. This members-only conference was open exclusively to SRC-affiliated engineers,
scientists, recruiters,and SRC Research Scholars. As SRC’s flagship event, TECHCON focused on semiconductor innovation, cutting-edge industry research, and workforce development,
bringing together academia and industry to shape the future of the microelectronics ecosystem.
